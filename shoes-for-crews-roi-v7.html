AR vs. containment

All projections use Automated Resolution (AR) — not containment. AR requires the AI to genuinely resolve the inquiry (relevant, accurate, safe, and contained). Containment simply measures whether a conversation avoided a handoff. Industry containment benchmarks are typically 15–30pp higher than AR. By using AR, this model reflects real customer outcomes.

sources

Chat data: Ada platform, CY 2025 — 103,345 opened, 54,659 engaged, 18,557 resolved (34.0% AR), 13,988 escalated (25.6%), 22,114 abandoned (40.5%).
Messaging AR: 500-conversation simulation on SFC knowledge base, no optimization (Ada, Sept 2025). Platform avg: ~80% AR.
Email AR: Set conservatively below messaging. 40% is an unoptimized starting point; typically improves 15–25pp within 90 days.
Voice AR: Most conservative. 20% targets automatable intents: order placement (45% of calls), shipping status, tracking.
Escalation model: Current ratio of escalated-to-unresolved (~39%) held constant, applied to smaller unresolved pool at projected AR.

All figures are illustrative and will vary based on implementation scope, configuration, and confirmed cost-per-contact. Projections represent conservative baselines — actual results typically improve with optimization and coaching.
